Lineage: MNDNR processing of precipitation data gathered by a variety of entities over 30 year period. Data sources include National Weather Service, DNR Forestry, Soil and Water Conservation Districts, and others. 300 to 1400 precipitation observers gathered data over 30 year period (seasonally varying).

Original method of collection was data surfaced with "Surfer" (Golden Software Inc.) application. 10 KM grids of monthly precipitation totals created for month-year using "Surfer". Data translated from "Surfer" format to MGC 100 standard using custom software. Original cell resolution: 0.22239 Acres. Minimum Mapping Unit: 10,000 meters. Date of Collection: 30 years of data, 1971-2000 - coverage prepared November 2002, modified July 2003.

Section 5: Attributes

Overview: GRIDCODE is an incremental index of the number 0.10 inch categories above 18.00 inches. For example, a GRIDCODE of 6 represents a range of normal annual precipitation values from 18.60" (MINVALUE) to 18.70" (MAXVALUE). A GRIDCODE of 163 represents a range of normal annual precipitation values from 34.30" (MINVALUE) to 34.40" (MAXVALUE).
